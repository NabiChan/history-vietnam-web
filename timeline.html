    • A Quick timeline about Vietnam history

    ~1200 BC - Đông Sơn Culture

    Bronze Age peak with famous drums and advanced craftmanship in Red River Delta.

    7th Century BC - Văn Lang Kingdom

    First Vietnamese state founded by Hùng Kings

    179 BC - Chinese Domination Begins

    Zhao Tuo conquered Âu Lạc, starting 1000 years of Northern rule

    40 AD - Trưng Sisters' Rebellion

    Trưng Sisters led revolt, became queens for 3 years. Symbol of Vietnamese resistance

    938 AD - Battle of Bạch Đằng

    Ngô Quyền defeated Chinese fleet, ending 1000 years of domination

    1010 AD - Lý Dynasty Golden Age

    Capital moved to Thăng Long. Temple of Literature founded. Vietnam prospered

    1258-1288 - Defeated Mongols

    Trần Dynasty defeated Mongol Empire three times under General Trần Hưng Đạo

    1428- Lê Lợi Victory

    Lam Sơn uprising defeated Ming, restored independence and prosperity

    1802- Nguyễn Dynasty

    Last Vietnamese dynasty founded by Gia Long Emperor

    1858-1884 - French Colonization

    France gradually conquered Vietnam despite fierce resistance

    1930 - Communist Party Founded

    Hồ Chí Minh unified communist parties, preparing for revolution

    September 2, 1945 - Independence

    Hồ Chí Minh declared independence after August Revolution

    19 December 1946 - 1 August 1954 - Battle againts France second colonization

    France begin to colonize Vietnam again

    1954 - Victory of Điện Biên Phủ

    Defeated France at Điện Biên Phủ, Vietnam is divided North-South at Geneva Accords

    1954-1975 - Vietnam War

    American began interfere in Vietnam

    1968 - Tết Offensive

    Major offensive shocked US forces, a turnng point for the war

    April 30, 1975 - Reunification

    Fall of Saigon. Vietnam unified under communist government

    1986 - Đổi Mới Reforms

    Economic renovation transformed Vietnam to market economy

    1995 - Globalization

    Joined ASEAN, normalized relations with America
