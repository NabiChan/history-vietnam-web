    After Vietnam War and challenges

    In 1976, Vietnam has officially united and rename to Socialist Republic of Vietnam with capital in Hanoi. On the next few years, private enterprises were seized by the goverment and their owners were often sent to the New Economic Zones to clear land. Transportation and storing food anđ goods privately was illegally by the goverment which caused shortage of food and goods combined with floods and disasters making a famine in the 1980's. Some of the illegally goods and foods were sold at the black market which often higher than their original price making the economy devastiated and the goverment failed to stop people trying to escape the country to other area illegally

    With the rising tension between Soviet and China and Vietnam was Soviet's ally which make to the future conflict of Vietnam with China and Cambodia, China's ally. Vietnam has also faced market embargo from the America and their allies

    Conflicts between Vietnam's neighbour countries

    War Againts Khmer Rouge (1978-1989)

    In 1975, Khmer Rouge seize over Cambodia. During the regime, they have did genocide to many of Cambodian, Vietnamese and intellectual class, they forced them to work labor even they took away their properties, executed people, destroying schools and hospitals. The goverment was also aided with weapons by China for againts Vietnam

    In May 1975, the Khmer Rouge launch an invasion to the Phú Quốc and Thổ Chu islands which were Vietnnam's territory, capturing and killing people on the islands. Meanwwhile, on land they also launch several attacks from 1975 to 1978 and one of the most brutal thing they have done is from 18 to 30 April 1978 they attack An Giang province of Vietnam and attack Ba Chúc village and they have killed more than 3000 innocent villagers there. Vietnamese army quickly respond and push the Regime's army to their border with Thailand and capture Cambodia's capital and saved millions of people there

    Sino-Vietnamese War 1979

    In late 1978, Cambodia has been counter-attacked by Vietnamese army to near border of Thailand. To respond and help their ally in Sotheast Asia, Jiang Zemin take reason to protect their border and "teach Vietnam a lesson" order Chinese armies attack the northern Vietnam from Lai Châu to Quảng Ninh. At the beginning of the war, Chinese has march to around 15-20 km from the border in Lạng Sơn, Cao Bằng, Lào Cai killing people and burn their house and stealing properties

    4/3/1979, Vietnam mobilized their troops to the north and counter attack Chinese troop. Facing the bravery army from Vietnam and has been counter attacked, from 6/3 to 11/3 in 1979 China announce to retreat their armies and Vietnamese order their troop not to attack them for peacefulness, however the Chinese troop on their way to retreat still destroy villages and stole properties and there still troops still stay in Vietnamese land illegally

    Although have retreated their military, from 18/3/1979 to late 1985 they still fire their artillery and attack the border causing heavy losses. In 28/5/1989, China launch second attack to the border with 500.000 troops and taken many lands from Vietnam. At the start of second invasion, Vietnamese troops hardly stand a chance to the heavy fire of artillery so they changed their plan to build trench to defense from heavy fire and to the year of 1989, China retreated their troop and made peace with Vietnam ending the 10-year war

    The Age Of Globalization

    Vietnam On The Road Of Recovery And Globalization

    Before 1986, Vietnamese economy has suffered from many wars and the lives of people was very poor, thus a Renovation must be held to tackle this problem. After the 6th National Congress, Vietnamese economy change from centralized planned economy to market based economy with Socialist-oriented

    Àfter the renovation was performed, the economy in Vietnam recovery and rose quickly. With the help of Agricultural reform, farmers allowed to sell their products to other provinces and countries making the food shortage ended and improve lives quality of people. The economy also rose quickly because they attact business and foreign investment to invest into facility and economics

    In 1991, Vietnam normalized relationship with China marking a breakthrough for Vietnam in the road of globalization. In the next few years, they have joined many global and local global organization. In 1995, Vietnam join ASEAN and in the same year they normalize and establish diplomatic relationship with America which make USA to stop the embargo. In 1977, they joined United Nation and in 2007 they joined World Trade Organization

    In 2025, Vietnam continue to mordenize and develop their economy and technology. Vietnam now hold an important position in cooperation in the global relationship and still aiming to become a developed and prosperous country
