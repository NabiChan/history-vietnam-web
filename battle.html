    First Indochina War(1945-1954)

    Battle againts colonization

    Begin the war

    On 13 September 1945, France with the help of Britain attack Saigon beggin the Second invasion of Vietanm. After that, they continue to took important places in the South and Southern Coast Vietnam. Vietnam has to make truce with both China and France but after some truces giving france control in some places but they continue to demand more and when they demand control over Hanoi, in 12/12/1946, Hồ Chí Minh made an Appeal for National Resistance and wrote that:"We want peace, we have to make concessions. But the more we make concessions, the more the French colonialists encroach on us because they want to take over our country again. No! We are better off sacrificing everything than losing our country and becoming slaves. Those who have guns will use guns. Those who have swords will use swords. If [you] don't have swords, then use hoes, spades, sticks and canes. Everyone must try to fight against the French colonialists to save our country."

    The first period of the war(1946-1951)

    On November 23 1946, French fleet bombarded the city of Haiphong killing 6000 Vietnamese innocents. In December 19, hostiles between French and Vietnam broke out in Hanoi and by December, Hồ Chí Minh and the goverment moved to the mountainous forests in Cao Bằng of Northern Vietnam

    In October 1947, France lauch operation Léa with the aim to taking out the main base of Vietnamese goverment and their battle units. At the beginning of operation, it cost Việt Minh (Vietanamese army) heavy losses and casualties. However, Việt Minh counter attack by lauching Việt Bắc Campaign using gurellia tactics to defeat the French armies making the France's operation failed.

    In 1950, after Communist China won the revolution they began to support Vietnamese front. In order to cut out supplyment line the Frenxh decided to evacuate Cao Bằng, which had been isolated, however the operation didnt went well and many of the French soldier got encircled and destroyed by Việt Minh and they retake the supply line making a disaster loss for the French

    Second period of the war(1951-1954)

    The Second Period of the Indochina War (1951-1954) was the decisive phase of Vietnam's fight for independence. During this time, the Việt Minh, under the leadership of Hồ Chí Minh and General Võ Nguyên Giáp, transformed into a stronger, more organized army. Key battles such as Hòa Bình (1951-1952) and the Northwest Campaign (1952) demonstrated growing Vietnamese strength. The turning point came with the historic Điện Biên Phủ Campaign in 1954, where the Việt Minh defeated the French fortress after 56 days of fierce fighting. This victory led to the Geneva Accords in July 1954, which ended French colonial rule in Indochina and temporarily divided Vietnam at the 17th parallel, setting the stage for the later Vietnam War

    Vietnam War (1954-1975)

    First Period (1954-1960)

    After Geneve Accords, Vietnam was divided into 2 goverments, the communist in the north supported by Soviet Union and China and the Ngô Đình Diệm in the south supported by United State after couping the Bảo Đại goverment.

    While the Ngô goverment in the south enforce 10/59 acts to executed the communist movement in all the south. The north carried out land reform from 1954 to 1960 to take the land from the landlord and give it to farmers and poor people. Although this land reform have some of the drawbacks but it strengthen the trust for the goverment

    Although according to Geneve Accords, Vietnam will be voted to unify the country but the South goverment broke the accords. To respond, the North create the Hồ Chí Minh Trial route to supply weapons to the revolutionaries in the South. In 1959, Communist Vietnam held the 15th Plenum of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of Vietnam and agree to use forces to overthrown the Diệm regime

    In morning17/1/1960, the Đồng Khởi movement broke out in 3 districts in Bến Tre and quickly spread to many regions in the South and the South goverment deploy military to attack the first 3 districts that the movement broke out, nevertheless, the attack failed even the military from the South has more modern military weapons and after that the movement spread even more. In the liberated era, the rural goverment was mostly destroyed and the land before Diệm's policy to reform the land was given back to farmers by the Đồng Khởi movement and this attracts more people joining this movement

    Second Period (1960-1968)

    From 1960 to 1968, North Vietnam moved from political support to full-scale war for reunification. After the Đồng Khởi movement, the National Liberation Front (NLF) was formed, and the Ho Chi Minh Trail became the key supply route to the South

    Following the Gulf of Tonkin Incident (1964), the U.S. launched Operation Rolling Thunder and sent combat troops to Vietnam. North Vietnam, aided by the Soviet Union and China, mobilized the entire country under a speech “All for the South, all for victory”

    Despite heavy bommbings, the Viet Cong has fought major battles and stand strong resisting the attack from the US and South Vietnam's army. In 1968, Tet Offensive had broke out in more than 100 cities, the USA not prepared and mark a turning point in the war. By the end of 1968, North Vietnam got many counter attacks but still resisting and even gained a major strategic advantage in the war

    Third Period (1968-1975)

    After Tết Offensive, the war entered a new phase. America gradually retreat troop back to America and use "Vietnamization" tactics and only support the South goverment with only weapons and they have to sit in the Paris treaty make peace

    Without American troops, the South Vietnamese army weaken. In battle of Đường 9 - Nam Lào (1971) and battle of Quảng Trị (1972) the Saigon regime heavyly losses even with US support marking the countdown of the last 3 years for the South goverment

    In January 1973, USA signed the Paris treaty and ending the military involvement in Vietnam. Taking advantage of it, North Vietnamese lanch the Spring Offensive in 1975 and they liberated quickly to all regions in Southern Vietnam, start with Buôn Ma Thuật to Huế, Đà Nẵng and finally in 30/4/1975, 2 tanks crash the gate of Independence Palace and hanging the Vietnam flag on top of the palace ending the war with the Vietnam unified under one Communist banner flag
