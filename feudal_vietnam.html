    Prehistoric

    First step in the form of a country

    Around hundreds of thousand years ago, Human sign has already appeared in Vietnam and scientist has found them in caves like Tham Hoi, Tham Khuyen in Lang Son, Đọ mountain in Thanh Hoa and early Vietnamese people survive by hunting and gathering

    In Stone and Bronze age human has learned to craft simple tools and cultivates wet rices expecially in the Bronze age when they have also decorated tools and ornaments

    Around 1200 B.C, the Đông Sơn culture has been formed accross the Mã River and Red River Delta which is the peak of Bronze age in Vietnam. The early humans decorate the Đông Sơn Drum with carvings and researcher also find weapons, jewelry and tools coming from this era emphasize the high level craftmanship in this era

    Văn Lang Dynasty and 1000 Years Of Chinese Domination

    The born of a special country

    The First Country

    The first known state of Vietnam, Văn Lang, was founded by the Hùng Kings around the 7th century BCE. Centered in the Red River Delta, it marked the beginning of Vietnamese civilization, with agriculture, bronze casting, and village organization forming the basis of early culture

    Văn Lang laid the foundation for the nation's identity and the long history of unity and resilience that defines Vietnam today

    Begin of 1000 years domination

    After the death of Qin Shi Huang, a former Qin general Zhao Tuo proclaim a new kingdom name Nanyue in the south of modern China and proclaim himself "King of Nanyue"

    In 179 BC, after many times of failing to conquer Âu Lạc kingdom he finally fulfil his campaign and defeated King An Dương Vương marking the 1000 years of Chinese or Northern domination

    First era of Northern Domination And Trung Sisters' Rebellions (111 BC-AD 43)

    In 111 BC, Han Dynasty conquer Nanyue kingdom and established its rule on new territories include Âu Lạc and rename its land to Giao Chỉ and make assimilation policy. Under the rule of Han Dynasty, Vietnamese people have to lived harsh life which lead to the Trung Sisters' Rebellions

    In 40 AD, Trung Sisters lead a successful againts the Han Dynasty. Trưng Trắc after angered by her husband killed lead the revolution with her sister and later become the queen or Trưng Nữ Vương but only 3 years later Han dynasty sent general Ma Yuan to invade again. After long and harsh battles, Trung Sisters commit sucide to avoid being capture but they have become the symbol of Vietnamese woman

    Second era of Northern Domination (43-554)

    After the defeat of Trưng Sisters, The Han Dynasty tighten their rules and spreading Chinese languages and culture and administration and the lives of Vietnamese people got worse

    During the fall of Han dynasty, Shi Xie create the Wu kingdom and after his death Giao Chỉ or Giao Châu become part of the kingdom. After the three kingdom era, Vietnam is ruled by continuous CHinese Dynasties

    In this era many uprising and revolution lead by Vietnamese people. The peak of revolution in this era were the Lady Triệu revolution lead by Lady Triệu but later the uprising failed and she commit sucided

    Establish of Vạn Xuân kingdom and Third era of Northern Domination(544- 905)

    In the end of Sui Dynasty, there were many revolts againts Chinese rules yet all of them ultimately fail. Untill Lý Bôn, his generals and heir Triệu Quang Phục successfully regain goverment in Vietnam and name the country Vạn Xuân independent for more than 50 years (544-602)

    However the country later in 602 invaded by Sui Dynasty and later ruled by Tang dynasty. During this period, Vietnamese people rebels againts the China rule in Vietnam typically the revolutions of Lý Tự Tiên, Đinh Kiến, Mai Hắc Đế, Phùng Hưng and Dương Thanh in VII to IX century

    Autonomus era and the end of 1000 years domination

    In 905, Khúc Thừa Dụ ruled Tĩnh Hải Quân incude vietnam marking the start of autonomous era during weakened China rule

    In 923, Southern Han invaded Tĩnh Hải Quân but was repelled by Dương Đình Nghệ. But after in 938, the Southern Han invaded again with their naval fleet power and general Ngô Quyền defeated their armies fleet by luring their armies into set up massive stakes in the Bạch Đằng river and when the tides fall, The Southern Han fleets sunken and half of their army was killed include their general. After the victory, Ngô Quyền become King Ngô and established a goverment in Cổ Loa marking the began of independence and dynastic period in Vietnam

    Dynastic period before French colonization(939-1858)

    Ngô, Đinh, and Early Lê dynasties (939-1009)

    After 6 years since Ngô Quyền become king, he was dead which lead to the power struggle for the throne result in the first major civil war in Vietnam or the upheaval of the Twelve Warlords (Loạn 12 Sứ Quân). This war lasted from 944 to 968 untill Đinh Bộ Lĩnh defeat other clans and proclaim himself Đinh Tiên Hoàng or Đinh the Majestic Emperor

    However in 979, Đinh Tiên Hoàng and his eldest son got assinated and only his 6 year old son survive and claim the throne. Take advantages of that the Song Dynasty invade Vietnam facing the threat of fall into China domination once again. Facing that threat, General Lê Hoàn took the throne he trick the enemies to the Chi Lăng Pass. The enemies fall into his ttrap and his armies ambush and also killed the Song general of this battle in 981

    After the Song withdrew their troop, Lê Hoàn was refered to his realm as Emperor Lê Đại Hoành. But in 1005, his death lead to the fighting between his sons for the throne and the winner is Lê Long Đĩnh, a tyrant king in Vietnam history. He devised many harsh punishments for the prisoners and people who againts him just for his entertainment. He died at the age of 24 in 1009 and he was so ill that he had to lie down while helding court. Then a palace guard name Lý Công Uẩn was nominated by the court and take the thone founded the Lý Dynasty

    Lý, Trần and Hồ Dynasty (1009-1407)

    The Lý Dynasty was the golden age of Dynastic period in Vietnam. In 1010, Lý Công Uẩn issued the Edict on the Transfer of the Capital, moving the capital Đại Cồ Việt from Hoa Lư to Thăng Long making it the political centre of Vietnam

    Later Emperors of the Dynasty continue to acclompish many of the good policies and built many things. The most significant things the rulers have made is the establish of Văn Miếu Quốc Tử Giám or Temple Of Literature, the first noble university in Vietnam, build dike systems for the farmers and held examinations to select elites for goverment positions annually. The Lý dynasty also have faced 2 invaders one from Song Dynasty in 1075, 1076 and one from Khmer Empire and Champa in 1128 and 1132 but all of the invaders failed to invade and defeated with heavy cost

    In 1225, the Lý Dynasty has ended when Empress Lý Chiêu Hoàng was arranged to marry and transfer the throne to Trần Cảnh founding the Trần Dynasty. During the Trần Dynasty, Vietnam has to faced the threat from the Mongol Empire And Mongol Yuan, the strongest empire in that time and Trần Dynasty not only successfully defense its territories once but three times in 1258, 1285, and 1287-88. Three times, the Empire that conquered most of Asia and big part of Europe have to fail and all thanks to the brilliant leadership of Trần Hưng Dạo and the bravery of his soldiers using guerrilla tactics and advantages of terrains crushing the ambition of Mongol to invade Vietnam and strengthen the indepeendent and glorious position in Vietnam history.

    However in 1400, the Trần Dynasty has fallen due to corrupt and power struggles and a court officals name Hồ Quý Ly overthrown the throne and make the Hồ Dynasty. The dynasty changed the country name to Đại Ngu and introduced a lot of progressive, military reforms but his overthrown give Ming Dynasty a reason to invade. In 1407 the Dynasty ended and marking the start of another 20 years of Chinese Domination in Vietnam

    Fourth Chinese Domination and Later Lê dynasty (1407-1527)

    During the China rules, they used assimilation policy to find way to erase Vietnamese culture and language. Vietnamese people have to faced high taxes, forced labors and Vietnamese books and historical records has been burnt or destroyed

    But then in 1428, Lê Lợi, son of a aristocrat, lead the Lam Sơn uprising againts Ming in Lam Sơn (Thanh Hoá) with the help of his general Nguyễn Trãi they have overcome many obstacles and setbacks but later on gain advancement and in battle of Chi Lăng they ambushed and killed commander Liu Shan.

    After the Ming troops surrenders and withdraw and in April 1428, Lê Lợi finally regain independent for Vietnam and established the Later Lê Dynasty becoming Emperor Lê Thái Tổ. The Later Lê Emperors brought Vietnam into another prosperous era, expecially Emperor Lê Thánh Tông was considered one of the greatest rulers in Vietnam expand to the South capturing Champa to Lê Dynasty territory. Literature and arts also flourished in this era. But later his death, the Dynasty slowly collapse due to power struggle and weaken rulers. In 1527, Mạc Đăng Dung killed the emperor marking the start of Decentralized period

    Decentralized period to before the French colanization(1527-1858)

    After overthrowing the Lê Dynasty, Mạc Đăng Dung founded the Mạc Dynasty (1527-1592) and became emperor. In the south, Nguyễn Kim, a former Lê official, led loyalists to restore the Lê rule

    Later, Trịnh Tùng of the Trịnh clan recaptured Hanoi and executed the Mạc king, beginning the long Trịnh-Nguyễn civil war. The Lê Dynasty was restored but held no real power, serving only as a puppet of the Trịnh Lords

    Vietnam was divided into 2 clans, the Trịnh clan control the North or Đàng Trong and the Nguyễn control the south or Đàng Ngoài. In 1627, take reason Nguyễn Phúc Nguyên refused to submit his authorities, Trịnh Tráng son of Trịnh Tùng sent armies to the south but only leaad to failure even have stronger armies and economic. The civil war lasted from 1627 until 1672 2 sides fights againts each other in many battles but got no total victory which lead to a truce in 1672. This civil war also gave european trade opportunities to trade weapons to each sides during this conflict

    In 1771, 3 brothers named Nguyễn Nhạc, Nguyễn Lữ and Nguyễn Huệ lead the Tây Sơn revolution in Quy Nhơn, which was in Nguyễn lord control. The revolution attract many people expecially poor people, farmers, workers because they have to faced heavy taxes, corruption in the mid 18th century under Nguyễn and Trịnh Clan control. By 1776, Tây Sơn has occupied all lands of Nguyễn Lord's and killed almost entire royal family and only prince Nguyễn Ánh survived, who fled to Siam to get military supported

    Nguyễn Ánh return with the help of 50.000 Siamese troops to regain power but was defeated then Nguyễn Ánh fled to Vietnam and he diddnt gave up. In 1786 to 1788, Tây Sơn army march north to fight Trịnh lord and they won total victory when capture the capital in less than 2 months. However, Lê Chiêu Thống, last Lê emperor, fled to Qing to ask for help in 1788 and they sent 200.000 armies to help regain his throne

    In December 1788, Nguyễn Huệ declear himself Quang Trung Emperor and defeat 100.000 Qing army during lunar new year in just 7 days. In 1792, in the way of marching south, Quang Trung Emperor đied by an unknow reason. After Quang Trung's death, Tây Sơn court became unstable and fighting among the Emperor's sons and the eventually winner is Nguyễn Quang Toản becoming Cảnh Thịnh Emperor

    Meanwhile, Nguyễn Ánh seek the help of the French through a Catholic prelate named Pigneaux de Behaine, however, because of the French revolution they couldnt aid with military support. Even though Pigneaux still managed to help Nguyễn Ánh recaptured Saigon and Quy Nhơn. In 1801, Nguyễn Ánh captured Tây Sơn's capital in Phú Xuân and by 1802 he sieged Thăng Long (Hanoi) and executed Nguyễn Quang Toản and Tây Sơn's royals.

    After defeating Tây Sơn, Nguyễn Ánh decleared himself Gia Long Emperor marking the start of Nguyễn Dynasty (1802-1945). During early Nguyễn Emperor, they have built many roads, construct cannals, held examination and care facilities for the sick people, writing history books and drawing maps and most importantly telorated Christian and employs some European include French as Gia Long's advisor.

    Everything ended when Minh Mạng Emperor took the throne and he adopt conservative and 'close door' policies and brutally executed or suppresed Catholicism and this continue with continuous Emperors untill the French invasion of Vietnam. There were many uprising againts the Nguyễn's, the persecution of Catholics and the imposition of trade embargoes were soon used as excuses for France to invade Vietnam

    French colonial period (1862-1945)

    French conquest of Vietnam (1858-1887)

    In the year of 1857, Napoleon III created Committee de la Cochinchine with the target of invading Vietnam and also compete with British Empire on growing influence in Asia, and in the same year Tự Đức Emperor executed 2 Spanish priest. Meanwhile, France still have war with Qing in the Second Opium War thus they send troops and naval fleets alongside with Spanish in order to revenge for executed 2 Spanish priests

    On 1 September 1858, an armies of 14 warships and 3000 troops led by General Charles Rigault de Genouilly launch an attack in Đà Nẵng. Genouilly's plan is to take Đà Nẵng as fast as possible and attack Huế, capital of Đại Nam or Việt Nam that time, forcing them to surrender and the French thought Catholicism and priest will support them during the invasion but they diddnt. Franco-Spanish troops there have to face many diseases and strong resistance from the Nguyễn Dynasty armies and Vietnamese people expecially with the brilliant defensive leader of Nguyễn Tri Phương

    Unable to achieve a breakthrough, Rigault decided to move his troops toward the south into Saigon, rice basket of Đại Nam with purpose of cutting the food supply for the armies of Đại Nam and take the lower Mekong province. On 17 February 1859, they took citadel of Saigon within only 2 days and Nguyễn Tri Phương had to move to the south to fight the invaders.

    By 1860, France has to move its troops to fight Qing, leaving only 800 Frenchmen and 100 Spanish facing against 10,000 Vietnamese led by Nguyễn Tri Phương. However, Nguyễn Tri Phương hesitate to attack left-over French troops and instead built Đại Đồn Chí Hoà a strong defensive fortress. In February 1861, France has reinforced its military in Saigon broke through vietnamese defense line and capture Đại Đồn Chí Hoà. After victory in Đại Đồn Chí Hoà, France continue to take Mỹ Tho, Biên Hoà and Gia Định forcing Nguyễn Dynasty to sign Treaty of Saigon in 1862, cutting the 3 region that French invaded to the France with high war reparation

    Meanwhile, taking advantages of weaken Dynasty, Tạ Văn Phụng proclaim himself prince of Lê and revolts to restore the Lê Dynasty but the rebels failed afterward when Nguyễn Dynasty sign peace with France to put down the uprising

    To repay the war reperation, Tự Đức Emperor increase taxes and force labor for the people making the goverment more unstable and corrupted, even part of the money was also used to built Tomb of Tự Đức just in case he death. The situation for people in the three occupied southern Vietnam has also facing heavy taxes and work like a slavery for the French people and this led to the uprising and was led by Trương Định and people proclaim him Bình Tây Đại Nguyên Soái. ALthough he wasdead 2 years later, his son continuosly led the uprising for 6 more years and make France heavy casualties and lost.

    Take advantages of this, France accused Nguyễn Dynasty was responsible for the uprising and they have to repay the 3 left provinces in the south to France marking all of southern Vietnam fall into French colony and this also weaken the uprising and they failed later on. In 1873, a french trader name ean Dupuis selling illegally in the red river and the Dynasty call France to eject him. France responded by sending marines and sailors led by Francis Garnier to Hanoi but not to eject but to suprisingly attack. Even Nguyễn Tri Phương and his army bravery defense Thăng Long citadel but he was captured and he refused any help or money coming from France and he chose to starve to death. Even successfully captured Thăng Long the french armies face strong resistance from the dynasty and black flag and in battle of Cầu Giấy Garnier was death by being ambushed by the enemies.

    In 7/1883, taking chance after Tự Đức emperors was death France captured Đà Nẵng and Huế Citadel forcing the goverment of Đại Nam to sign Patenôtre treaty in 1884 marking the start of colonization era in all of the territories of Vietnam

    Revolutions and resistance movements before World War 2 (1887-1939)

    In 1885, Tôn Thất Huyết and teenage Emperor Hàm Nghi started the Cần Vương ("Save the Emperor") Movement rallying people againts the rule of the French colonization. With the help of Phan Đình Phùng this movement attract many people to stand under Cần Vương Movement flag made France lost many casualties and money. However this movement only last untill 1888 when Emperor was captured and exiled to Algeria

    Many of the later movement and revolution was ended with lost due to lack of modern weapons and equipment to resit the French. Vietnamese people in this era have to face even more taxes and discrimination in this era, although the France made 2 modernization progress for the transportation and facilities but they was almost all only served for the transportation of armies and resources for France people. France also deploy assimilation policy made children to learn French at school and taught that they are French and only teach French history.

    Coming to the 1900s, there were 2 significant movements. One is Đông Du or "Travel to the East" led by Phan Bội Châu and his plan was to send Vietnamese people to Japan to learn more about technology, modernization and weapons so in the future they could come back to Vietnam to led a revolution for the independent of Vietnam. However, this was ultimately failed due to growing relationship between France and the Japanese, France make diplomatic pressure which make Japan have to deported Châu

    Another movement is Duy Tân Movement led by Phan Châu Trinh, favoring a peaceful movement by stressing the importance of education in modernizing, developing economies and fostering understanding and tolerence between French and Vietnamese. In 1907, he and associates Lương Văn Can, Nguyễn Quyền opened a patriotic modern school name Tonkin Free School in Hanoi and the purpose of it is to open the vietnamese people mind without taking money and they adapt the use of Romanized Quốc Ngữ alphabelt to quickly educated people.

    After the 2 movements failed or didn't gain much result, Vietnamese movement started to shift to the radical path. Phan Bội Châu create another movememnt name Việt Nam Quang Phục Hội in Guangzhou planning armed resistance against the French. Unfortanately in 1925, he was captured in Shanghai and was took to Vietnam to executed but Vietnamese people resistance for sparing Phan Bội Châu make him only placed under house arrét till he dead in 1940. In 1927, there also Vietnamese Nationalist Party inspired by Kuomintang in China and they launched Yên Bái Mutiny in 10/02/1930 but they only last for 1 weeks before French capture the uprising leaders and executed

    Communism in this time was also introduced in this era with the 3 communist parties: the Indochinese Communist Party, Annamese Communist Party and the Indochinese Communist Union. To avoid any conflicts and strengthen relationship between 3 parties, Nguyễn Ái Quốc (Hồ Chí Minh) was sent to Hongkong to unified the 3 parties into only 1 party named Vietnamese Communist Party (CPV) with Trần Phú as the first Secretary General latered renamed to Indochinese Communist Party. The communist party led the rebelled Soviet Nghệ-Tĩnh in 1930-31 which was the peak of revolution and was an important step to the revolution in 1945 although the rebelled later failed

    Japanese Empire Invasion and October Revolution(1940-1945)

    In 1939, World War 2 has started and France quickly get lost in Europe and become the Vichy puppet goverment. Meanwhile, Japanese Empire sign Tripartite Pact alongside with Germany and Italy joining the war on the side of Axis and having a war with China warlords, seeking for resources and Stragetic location in Asia and Vietnam or Indochina was the first target. In 22/09/1940, Japanese armies march to Lạng Sơn and quickly annexed Vietnam but still keep French goverment in Vietnam and to this point Vietnamese people were stuck between 2 brutal collonial rule- The Japanese Empire and France

    Vietnamese people have to dealt with high taxes, forced labors from the 2 collonial rulers which make people want to make a revolution. Expecially in 1945, Japanese kicked France out of their colony and fully control Indochina

    Then the Japanese forced farmers to replace growing rice with jutes to support their war. By the same time, flood and disasters destroyed many farms and rice and the Japanese forced to take rice away from the farmers. These reasons lead to one of the most deadliest famine in the world from 1944-1945 and this took about 2 million people death in only the north which was about 10 to 15% of the population in the North Vietnam and even some villagers were fully wiped out just because of famine

    On August 1945, Hồ Chí Minh led the August revolution and quickly spread the revolution to all places in Vietnam. In September 2 1945, Hồ Chí Minh read the Proclamation of Independence of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam (DRV) in Ba Đình square and after many years, Vietnam finally got independence
