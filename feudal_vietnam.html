    Prehistoric

    First step in the form of a country

    Around hundreds of thousand years ago, Human sign has already appeared in Vietnam and scientist has found them in caves like Tham Hoi, Tham Khuyen in Lang Son, Đọ mountain in Thanh Hoa and early Vietnamese people survive by hunting and gathering

    In Stone and Bronze age human has learned to craft simple tools and cultivates wet rices expecially in the Bronze age when they have also decorated tools and ornaments

    Around 1200 B.C, the Đông Sơn culture has been formed accross the Mã River and Red River Delta which is the peak of Bronze age in Vietnam. The early humans decorate the Đông Sơn Drum with carvings and researcher also find weapons, jewelry and tools coming from this era emphasize the high level craftmanship in this era

    Văn Lang Dynasty and 1000 Years Of Chinese Domination

    The born of a special country

    The First Country

    The first known state of Vietnam, Văn Lang, was founded by the Hùng Kings around the 7th century BCE. Centered in the Red River Delta, it marked the beginning of Vietnamese civilization, with agriculture, bronze casting, and village organization forming the basis of early culture

    Văn Lang laid the foundation for the nation's identity and the long history of unity and resilience that defines Vietnam today

    Begin of 1000 years domination

    After the death of Qin Shi Huang, a former Qin general Zhao Tuo proclaim a new kingdom name Nanyue in the south of modern China and proclaim himself "King of Nanyue"

    In 179 BC, after many times of failing to conquer Âu Lạc kingdom he finally fulfil his campaign and defeated King An Dương Vương marking the 1000 years of Chinese or Northern domination
