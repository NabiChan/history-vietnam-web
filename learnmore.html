    The art of war in Vietnam history

    Throughout of the entire history of Vietnam, they have defeated many strong empires and countries, from Mongol Empire, China to France and United States. To won againts invaders, tactics play a very important role in the wars so lets summarize some of the most significant strategies in the history of this country. One of the most useful tactics in dynastic period in Vietnam was putting stakes under the river and lurring them to the river wait the water to fall down and destroy enemies naval and one of the significant battle using this was battle of Bạch Đằng river in 1228 made thousands of Yuan armies dead or captured

    Another tactics was Empty garden, when army destroy food and properties and move innocents away so that when the enemies came they didnt have any food and supply from the invaded land and it was used in 1258 againts Mongol Empire made the enemies suffered from food shortage and play an important role to win the battle. And the most used tactic strategy in vietnam was guerilla, when facing much more stronger enemies expecially againts United State in Vietnam war, Vietnamese soldier can not face them directly, instead they ambush the army from the bush or the forest and even the people who fought for america during this war have to admit that they were very scared when looking at any bush or trees because they dont know if they can speak vietnamese and shot AK-47

    Recommended Literatures and Books

    The 2 masterpieces of Vietnamese' literature

    To know more about history of Vietnam, we cannot only look from lectures from school or university but also books to get better understanding about the lives of the people reflected in the books and litearatures work so here are the 2 recommended books to read

    The first one is The Tale of Kiều, an poem wrote by Nguyễn Du recounts the life, trials and tribulations of Thúy Kiều, a beautiful and talented young woman, who has to sacrifice herself to save her family by selling herself into marriage with a middle-aged man, not knowing that he is a pimp, and is forced into prostiuation. This masterpiece reflects the fate of Vietnamese people expecially woman when they have to face many obstacles but instead of giving up they still stand up to live on for a better life!

    The second one is Red Rain wrote by Chu Lai, this literature took place in battle of Quảng Trị in Vietnam war and every of the harsh and brutal of the war was shown in the book. Even the enemies have much more modernized weapons and air power when Vietnamese soldier have to live in the trench to keep Quảng Trị citadel from the enemy in 81 days although it not as long as other war but the Vietnamese soldier have to face even more bombs than the World War 2 combined. This book gratitude to all soldiers that fought in the war so that the future generation can have independence and happy lives right now and this book also have a movie with the same name in 2025 in Vietnam

    The story of a Human

    Hồ Chí Minh on the road finding indepence for Vietnam

    On 6 May 1911, in Nhà Rồng harbour, a young person name Nguyễn Tất Thành get on the Amiral Latouche-Tréville ship with the job being the chef helper with a wish to find a way to make Vietnam become independence. The ship arrived in France on 5 July 1911 then the ship arrived to Dunkirk and return to France on mid-September and here he applied to a Colonial school but got rejected so he deciđe to visit more countries in 1911 include United state and Britain

    Then he returned to France from around 1917-1919 and in here he has been publishing newspaper for Vietnamese independence and their civil right under a name called Nguyễn Ái Quốc. In treaty of Versailles, Nguyễn Ái Quốc send the Demands of the Annamite People outlining eight points to achieve basic rights for the Vietnamese people under French colonial rule although it was rejected by global powers, this was a strike to the future fall of colonization. In 7/1920, he read The Preliminary Draft of Theses on the Colonial Question by Lenin and till that time he has finally found the ideology to make Vietnam become independent from France

    In the later years, he come to Soviet and China to study about Marxism-Leninism and organize the Vietnamese Revolutionary Youth Association publishing newspapers called Thanh Niên. In 1930, he united the 3 Communism party in Vietnam marking a breakthrough in the road to independent of Vietnam. He later returned to Vietnam in 1941 with the name Hồ Chí Minh founded Việt Minh front and led the August Revolution in 1945, declearing independence for Vietnam after many years of being colanized
